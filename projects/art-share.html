Art Share Site

Mar 2025 - Jun 2025 - Personal Project

GitHub Repo: https://github.com/Callum-OP/Art-Media-App



Django, Python, Angular, TypeScript, HTML, CSS, SQLite, Docker

A social media site designed for sharing art or animation files, uses Django for backend and Angular for frontend. Developed in my own time after learning Django.

Features:
  • Create, Read, Update and Delete functionality for posts and comments.
  • Secure login tokens.
  • User accounts can be created and edited.
  • Users can be followed and posts can be liked.
  • Can also search for posts by title or description or filter posts by newest or most liked.
  • If user is not logged in they can view posts and comments but not add them.
